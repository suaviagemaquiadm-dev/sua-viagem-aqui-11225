# **App Name**: Sua Viagem AQUI

## Core Features:

- Destination Search: Allows users to search for events, tours and partners by destination and date.
- Category Filters: Filter and find experiences based on a predefined category list (agencies, lodging, restaurants).
- Partner Listing: Display partners and advertisers including premium agencies in a carousel.
- Subscription Plans: Offer subscription tiers (free, basic, plus, advance) to allow partners to promote their services and access a wider audience.
- Itinerary Builder: Generate personalized trip itineraries for the agency's clients based on AI to analyze a description of customer profiles and travel preferences, providing complete itineraries to generate qualified leads for businesses on the platform. AI will act as a tool to assist trip designers.
- Traveler Plans: Subscription plans for travelers.
- Partner Profile: Enables partners and businesses to create profiles to showcase what they offer to travelers on the platform.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Amber (#FCD34D) to evoke feelings of energy, warmth, and trust.
- Background color: Dark slate (#1A202C) creates a professional and trustworthy feel.
- Accent color: Light orange (#FCA5A5) provides contrast and emphasizes important sections.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', sans-serif, known for clarity and modernity. Suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use Font Awesome for clear and recognizable icons across the platform.
- Glassmorphism and layered elements with shadows enhance visual depth.
- Subtle animations on interactive elements such as hover states on the Premium card.
